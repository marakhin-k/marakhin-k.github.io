About

Born in Kuban in 1991, lives and works in Moscow. Graduated from the Russian Academy of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture (2016), studied under A. Smirnov-Panfilov and S. Shcherbakov. Member of the Moscow Union of Artists. Collaborated with leading Russian sculptors — S. Shcherbakov, D. Namdakov, A. Kovalchuk.

In my work, I explore the duality of human nature, transforming classical form through dialogue with material — bronze, patina, texture. Nature is a sacred space for me, a reflection of inner states. Works have been exhibited in Moscow and Indonesia.
